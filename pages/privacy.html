1. Introduction

Welcome to Rawfin ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website rawfin.tv, use our podcast platform, interact with our Telegram bot (@rawfin_bot), or authenticate through third-party services.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at support@rawfin.tv.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, participate in activities on the website, or otherwise contact us.

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures, bio, and preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, and correspondence you send to us
  • Newsletter Data: Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment processors (we do not store credit card details)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, podcast listening history
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Information stored in cookies and similar technologies (see Section 6)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To facilitate account creation, authentication, and management
  • Content Delivery: To provide you with podcast episodes and personalized recommendations
  • Communications: To send you newsletters, updates, and marketing materials (you can opt out at any time)
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our platform and improve our services
  • Security: To protect our services, detect fraud, and ensure platform integrity
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide support

4. Telegram Bot Data Collection

When you interact with our Telegram bot (@rawfin_bot), we collect and process the following information:

4.1 Information Collected from Telegram

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram identifier
  • Username: Your Telegram username (if publicly available)
  • Display Name: Your Telegram display name
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to the bot for processing
  • Interaction Data: Commands used, buttons clicked, and interaction timestamps

4.2 How We Use Telegram Data

  • Provide bot functionality and respond to your commands
  • Send you notifications about new podcast episodes and market updates
  • Link your Telegram account to your Rawfin account (if you choose to authenticate)
  • Improve bot performance and user experience

4.3 Telegram Data Retention

We retain your Telegram data for as long as you use the bot. You can request deletion at any time by sending /delete command or contacting support@rawfin.tv.

5. OAuth Authentication

Rawfin offers authentication through multiple third-party providers. When you choose to log in using OAuth, we access limited information from these providers:

5.1 OAuth Providers We Support

  • Twitter/X: Profile information, email address
  • Facebook: Name, email address, profile picture
  • GitHub: Username, email address, profile picture
  • Discord: Username, discriminator, email address, avatar
  • LinkedIn: Name, email address, profile picture

5.2 Information We Receive from OAuth Providers

When you authenticate through an OAuth provider, we receive:

  • Your unique identifier from that provider
  • Your email address (if made available by the provider)
  • Your public profile information (name, username, profile picture)
  • Any other information you explicitly grant us permission to access

5.3 How We Use OAuth Data

  • Create or link your Rawfin account
  • Authenticate you when you log in
  • Populate your profile information
  • Send you service-related communications

Note: We do not have access to your OAuth provider passwords. Authentication is handled securely by the respective providers.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and store information about your preferences and interactions with our platform.

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality (authentication, security)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent)

6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Data Sharing

We only share your information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

7.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Cloud hosting providers (for data storage)
  • Analytics providers (to understand platform usage)
  • Email service providers (for newsletters and communications)
  • Payment processors (for subscription payments)
  • OAuth providers (Twitter, Facebook, GitHub, Discord, LinkedIn)
  • Telegram (for bot functionality)

7.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

7.3 Business Transfers

If Rawfin is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

8. Data Retention

We will only keep your personal information for as long as it is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion request
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 24 months for analytics purposes
  • Legal Data: Retained as required by applicable laws and regulations

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

10.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

10.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access personal information
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to equal service and price, even if you exercise your privacy rights

10.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@rawfin.tv. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
